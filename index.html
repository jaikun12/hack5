Say hello to Thriftr.

"Find what you need, easier and cheaper."

Thriftr is a web application that allows you to search for markets near you that offers the cheapest price for the produce you are looking for.

Want to know where you can find the produce you want in the cheapest price? Pick your location and type the produce you want to learn where you can find it at the cheapest price.

Why was it made?

Thriftr was our entry for Hackercup, De la salle University's Hackathon held last November 15, 2015. The theme of the hackathon was to utilize data provided by the government through their portal data.gov.ph

The application helped us in obtaining the fifth place in our category, where over 25 apps were judged by a panel of professionals.

How did we get the prices?

The market data on prices were obtained from the data set provided by the Department of Agriculture here in the Philippines. The data set could be found here.
